Cakravala Method

The Cakravāla method is a cyclic algorithm to solve indeterminate quadratic equations of type a2 = N*b2 + 1, attributed to Bhāskara II. Our application produces the values of a and b and the verbose option produces the intermediate steps. For more information, go to Cakravala Method.
