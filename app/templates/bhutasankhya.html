Bhūtasaṅkhyā System

Bhūta in Sanskrit means entity or thing. The word bhumi (earth) and its synonyms represent number 1, the word akṣi (eye) and its synonyms represent number 2, and so on. Since we have many synonyms for each word in the Sanskrit language, it becomes very easy to use a suitable word for the chosen meter to represent numbers. Our application handles the decoding and encoding of this system. For decoding, it parses the given string when more than one words are present and gives the resulting number. For encoding, it retrieves the matching words from the database of over 800 Sanskrit words for the input number.

Examples for the decoder:
  • नागवेदनभसप्तरामाङ्कस्वरभूमि
  • वेदवेदाङ्गचन्द्र
